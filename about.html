Our Story

Founded in 2020 by Hammam Aghbar, H1APPS began as a small team of passionate developers with a vision to create impactful mobile applications. Today, we've grown into a full-service mobile app development company serving clients worldwide.

Our journey started with a single project and has since expanded to over 50 successful app launches across various industries. We pride ourselves on delivering high-quality, user-centric solutions that drive business growth.

Our Mission

To empower businesses and individuals through innovative mobile solutions that solve real-world problems and enhance user experiences.

Our Vision

To be a leading mobile app development company recognized for excellence, creativity, and technological innovation in Palestine and beyond.
